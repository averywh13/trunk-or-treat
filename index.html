Date: October 29, 2024

Location: Marlboro High School Parking Lot

Come join Marlboro High School's Trunk-or-Treat for ages 12 and under. All donations will be collected for the American Cancer Society. Please ensure no food containing nuts as this is a nut-free event. Face painting and vendors such as "Feds on the Go" and "Goldy's Ice Cream" will be provided. Bring your scariest costume and an empty bag for a spooky trunk-or-treat event!
